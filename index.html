Stay Safe. Stay Smart. Stay Strong.

Essential information for prevention and mental wellbeing

Prevention Tips

😷

Wear a Mask

Properly fitted masks reduce transmission risk significantly.

🧼

Wash Hands

Frequent hand washing with soap for at least 20 seconds.

↔️

Maintain Distance

Keep at least 6 feet distance from others in public.

💉

Get Vaccinated

Vaccines are safe and effective against severe illness.

🧪

Get Tested

If symptomatic or exposed, get tested immediately.

🏠

Stay Home

When sick to prevent spreading illness to others.

Myth vs Fact

Myth

"COVID-19 doesn't affect young people."

Fact

While risk increases with age, young people can still experience severe illness, especially with variants.

Myth

"Hot weather stops COVID-19."

Fact

The virus can spread in all climates, regardless of temperature or weather.

Myth

"Vaccines contain microchips."

Fact

Vaccines contain only ingredients to stimulate immune response, no tracking devices.

Mental Health Support

"Your mind matters too."

The pandemic has been stressful for everyone. Here are resources to help:

  • WHO Mental Health Toolkit: Download Guide
  • National Helpline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Mindfulness Apps: Headspace, Calm, Insight Timer

Tips for Coping:

  • Maintain a daily routine
  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Limit news consumption
  • Practice relaxation techniques
  • Get regular exercise
